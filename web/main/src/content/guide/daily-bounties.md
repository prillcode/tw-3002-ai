# Daily Bounties

Every day, CHOAM posts three bounties. Complete them for credit rewards.

## How It Works

- **3 missions per day**, rotating at **UTC midnight**
- Press **B** from any sector to open your bounty panel
- Progress tracks automatically as you play
- Claim rewards instantly — no need to return to a port

## Mission Types

| Type | What You Do | Typical Target |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Kill NPCs** | Destroy pirate ships in combat | 2–15 kills |
| **Trade Credits** | Profit from port transactions | 10,000–50,000 credits |
| **Visit Sectors** | Warp to new sectors | 3–20 sectors |
| **Claim a Planet** | Colonize an unclaimed planet | 1–2 planets |
| **Pay Taxes** | Pay CHOAM tariffs in FedSpace | 1–5 payments |

## Difficulty Tiers

Each mission is ranked **Easy**, **Medium**, or **Hard**:

| Tier | Chance | Reward Range | Target Range |
|------|--------|--------------|--------------|
| **Easy** | 40% | 300–1,000 credits | Low targets |
| **Medium** | 35% | 800–2,500 credits | Moderate targets |
| **Hard** | 25% | 1,500–5,000 credits | High targets |

> **Pro tip:** Easy *Pay Taxes* missions are the fastest credits in the game. Always pay tariffs in FedSpace — it counts toward your bounty.

## Rerolling

Don't like a mission? Reroll it for credits.

- **Cost:** `max(500, floor(reward × 0.5))`
- A 1,000-credit mission costs **500** to reroll
- A 5,000-credit mission costs **2,500** to reroll
- You can only reroll **uncompleted, unclaimed** missions
- The replacement mission is the **same difficulty tier**

> **Strategy:** Reroll impossible missions (e.g., *Claim a Planet* when you're poor). Keep easy credits.

## Claiming Rewards

1. Open the bounty panel (**B**)
2. Look for the green **Claim** button on completed missions
3. Click to deposit credits immediately

> **Note:** You must claim rewards the same day. Missions reset at UTC midnight.

## Progress Tracking

Your progress updates in real time:
- Killing an NPC increments **Kill NPCs**
- Trading profit increments **Trade Credits**
- Warping to a new sector increments **Visit Sectors**
- Colonizing a planet increments **Claim a Planet**
- Paying CHOAM tariffs increments **Pay Taxes**

Toast notifications appear when you complete a mission during gameplay.

## CHOAM Bounty Leaderboard

Your lifetime claimed bounty total appears on the **CHOAM Bounty** leaderboard tab. The more bounties you complete, the higher you rank.

> **Daily habit:** Check your bounties first thing. They shape how you play that day — a *Trade Credits* mission means focus on ports; *Kill NPCs* means hunt pirates.
