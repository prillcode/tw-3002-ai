## Alignment Mechanics
- Alignment values range from **‑99** to **‑1** for evil players.  
- Evil alignment removes federation protection and grants unrestricted access to underground sectors.  

## Aggressive Economic Tactics

### Sell‑Steal‑Move (SSM)

| Requirement                | Value                         |
|----------------------------|------------------------------|
| Minimum alignment          | ‑100 or lower                |
| Minimum experience         | 1,000 exp (recommended)      |
| Experience needed for safe operation | `Holds × 20` exp (minimum 15 exp) |

> **Note:** Holds refers to the number of cargo holds you have installed on your ship.

## Rob and Steal Settings

Two server‑wide settings govern how much you can **rob** credits and **steal** holds from a port:

| Setting      | Description                              | Default (MBBS) | Default (Classic) |
|--------------|------------------------------------------|----------------|-------------------|
| **RobFactor**   | Percentage applied to the robbery formula | 50 %          | 50 % (variable)   |
| **StealFactor**| Percentage applied to the steal formula   | 70 %          | 30 % (variable)   |
